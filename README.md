# Guard AI

Guard AI is an AI-based system that helps Indian youth avoid risky financial decisions.

Many young people chase quick money through trading or betting and hide financial struggles due to fear of judgement. Guard AI silently detects risky intentions and gives private alerts and safer suggestions.

## Goal
To reduce financial mistakes and protect youth from scams or debt.

## Status
Hackathon idea-stage project.
